Baaraakatta – Account Deletion Request

You can request deletion of your Baaraakatta account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

Send an email to saiprakash.nalubolu@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request – Baaraakatta
  • Your display name or email used to sign in

We will delete your account and data within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials
  • Your display name and profile
  • Any game history associated with your account

What is retained

  • Anonymous aggregated game statistics (not linked to you)
